Welcome to the RGU Death Metal Society

Do you want to spend time making music that will make your neighbour’s ears bleed? Their children cry and their invaluable china shatter?
...Then what are you waiting for? Come join us at the RGU Death Metal Society!!

Our Mission:
Our Mission is exploring our common passion for Metal and sharing it with one another. This includes everything from Death Metal to Classic Rock, Blues, Blues, Progressive, Thrash and everything in between. Our passion for Metal extends to creating our own, with many of our members emphasising experimental and innovative sounds that will push the boundaries of the genre.

Snot's New Album!

Snot, one of our oldest ongoing projects is excited to anounce that they're first album, 'Get Some' is in the final stages of production. Snot are setting the release date at late this year, early next year. For a sample track visit our projects page, which will also provide you with information on how to support Snot. The album is not available for preorder yet, however, the band will inform the Society as soon as they have a date for that too!

Dillinger Escape Plan

Dillinger Live Shows!

Dillinger Escape Plan, another of the Societies major projects, also have big news. The band recently started working on their first album, 'Calculating Infinity', no release date has been announced yet - It's expected that the album won't be ready for a couple of years. However, in the meantime Dillinger announced that they'll be performing Live at a variety of different locations across Scotland. A location and date for Aberdeen have been set - visit our Events page for more info. If you want more information on Dillinger, visit our Projects page.

Current Club House

Donations for New Club House

With an increasing amount of our Society members looking to start their own projects, or just looking for a place to jam with friends. We've decided to invest into a new Club House. Although our current one has served us well, space and equipment are limited. We're looking to collect donations to place down a deposit for a new one. Our plan is to invest into new equipment and editing software along with the club house, making it easier for all memebers to create their own music. We're considering keeping both club houses, making it easier for members to rent time in them. However, that will depend on the amount collected. To place donation or for any further enquires, contact Dave Lombardo.
